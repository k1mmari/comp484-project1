Jujutsu Kaisen

術式Jutsushiki

Cursed Techniques are abilities fueled by cursed energy. Different types include innate techniques, barrier techniques, shikigami, new shadow style, and more.

Technique Activation

A cursed technique is activated by pouring cursed energy into it using one of two processes: Lapse or Reversal. If cursed energy is comparable to electricity, cursed techniques are the appliances that use that power to function.

順転Junten

Lapse refers to the default state of activation for a cursed technique. The caster circulates their cursed energy forward to channel it into their ability.

反転Hanten

Reversal generates positive energy by multiplying negative energy with negative energy. Negative energy can’t heal, but positive energy can recover physical injuries.

My Fave Techniques

十種影法術Tokusa no Kage Bojutsu

The Ten Shadows Technique is an inherited Zen’in Clan ability that allows its wielder to summon and command up to ten shikigami using shadows as a medium. Each shikigami must first be exorcised before it can be tamed, forcing the user into life-or-death battles to claim them. Once bound, these familiars can emerge from darkness at will, fighting alongside their master or being combined in creative ways. If a shikigami is destroyed, its strength and abilities are absorbed into the remaining ones, allowing the technique to evolve with every loss.

What makes the Ten Shadows so feared is not just its versatility but its untapped potential. Among its familiars lies Mahoraga, a divine beast said to adapt to any phenomenon or attack—a power so overwhelming that no sorcerer has ever fully controlled it. Though outcast by his clan, Megumi Fushiguro wields the technique with unorthodox creativity, treating his shikigami as partners. His ingenuity hints that Ten Shadows could one day rival the legendary techniques of the Gojo Clan.

無下限Mukagen

The Limitless Technique is the Gojo Clan’s inherited ability that manipulates space using the concept of infinity. Attacks slow to a halt before reaching the user as distances are endlessly divided. This passive defense makes the sorcerer nearly untouchable, but Limitless also enables powerful applications.

**Blue** compresses space to attract, **Red** repels with overwhelming force, and **Hollow Purple** annihilates anything in its path by collapsing existence. At peak mastery, **Unlimited Void** overwhelms targets with infinite information. With the Six Eyes reducing energy cost, Gojo Satoru alone has wielded Limitless to its full potential.

呪霊操術Jurei Sojutsu

The Cursed Spirit Manipulation Technique allows the user to absorb and command cursed spirits. After exorcising a spirit, the technique consumes its essence and binds it to the user’s will. Bound spirits retain their abilities and can be summoned as an arsenal tailored to each battle.

Suguru Geto used this technique to amass thousands of curses, from fodder to special grades. By combining spirits and layering abilities, he exploited enemy weaknesses and overwhelmed even elite sorcerers—making him a strategic counterpart to Gojo’s overwhelming force.
